Centeralized Digital Toolkit

Overview

This toolkit application designed to communicate to all the installations in the experience center.


It connects to either the centeralized server of the experience center or can connect to individual installations based on dedicated IP addresses.


Presenter can change and configure content on-the-go by selection configuration in the admin mode. Once setup, the presenter can select the viewer mode and showcase the relevant content on the installations.


This toolkit is highly modulder and customizable, and can be configured for different experience centers./p>

Kanakia Arcade Gaming Zone

Overview

A 1500 square meter arcade zone designed for kids. This zone consists of multiple installations designed for kids to play games and spend free time.


Proposed installations consists of Interactive Sketch Aquarium, Cycle Simulator, Interactive Table Tennis and Interactive Billiards.


In Interactive Sketch Aquarium, kids are given line art papers, and crayon colors. They color on their likings and then scan the paper. After completion, the custom application detects the QR code and selects modal based on the database. Once detected, the fish character appears based on their colors. Kids can interact with their characters by touching a large interactive wall. Initially for prototyping Kinect and IR touch overlay was used, which was later replaced by TUIO sensor because the length of the wall was 18 feet. This wall was multi touch, so multiple kids can interact with the game.


Interactive table tennis and billiards, are computer vision based smart tables where the custom high fps cameras are placed on the ceilings, and a projection is done on the flat surface of the table using projectors. The custom application created using game engine, detects the ball using blob detection technology, and also detects the touch of the user by understanding IR grid based layer on the table.

JSW - Museum of Solutions

Overview

Leveraging different technologies, the museum for kids was designed, that helped kids to understand the essance of water. This museum is one of it's kind in which, the theme of the museum changes every year like Water, Fire, Space, Air, etc. Currently the theme is water. The installations are designed in such a way that it can adapt any theme with minor changes in it. Multiple installations were proposed over the span of 3 years, and out of which most of them were selected by the clients. I was involved in the process of design research to ideations, prototyping to testing and finally execution on the site.
